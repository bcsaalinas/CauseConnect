Sustainable Development Goals

  1. No Poverty: Eradicate poverty in all forms everywhere.
  2. Zero Hunger: End hunger and ensure food security through sustainable agriculture.
  3. Good Health: Guarantee healthy lives and well-being for all ages.
  4. Quality Education: Provide inclusive and equitable education for everyone.
  5. Gender Equality: Achieve equality and empower all women and girls.
  6. Clean Water & Sanitation: Ensure safe water and sanitation access for all.
  7. Affordable & Clean Energy: Provide sustainable, modern, and reliable energy.
  8. Decent Work & Growth: Promote inclusive economic growth and decent jobs.
  9. Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure: Build resilient infrastructure and foster innovation.
  10. Reduced Inequalities: Decrease inequality within and among countries.
  11. Sustainable Cities: Make cities inclusive, safe, and environmentally friendly.
  12. Responsible Consumption: Ensure sustainable production and consumption patterns.
  13. Climate Action: Take urgent action to combat climate change.
  14. Life Below Water: Conserve oceans and marine resources.
  15. Life on Land: Protect forests, biodiversity, and ecosystems.
  16. Peace, Justice & Institutions: Promote peace, justice, and strong institutions.
  17. Partnerships for the Goals: Strengthen global cooperation for sustainability.
