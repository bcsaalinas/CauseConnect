Sustainable Development Goals

Explore the United Nations blueprint for a fairer, greener, and more inclusive world. Each goal below includes a quick note so readers understand the focus at a glance.

People Planet Prosperity
01

No Poverty

End poverty in all forms everywhere so families can live with dignity and security.

02

Zero Hunger

Achieve food security, improve nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture.

03

Good Health

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for people of all ages.

04

Quality Education

Guarantee inclusive and equitable education with lifelong learning opportunities.

05

Gender Equality

Empower women and girls and remove barriers that limit equal participation.

06

Clean Water & Sanitation

Provide safe water, sanitation, and hygiene for every community.

07

Affordable & Clean Energy

Expand access to reliable, modern, and sustainable energy sources.

08

Decent Work & Growth

Promote sustained economic growth and decent jobs for everyone.

09

Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

Build resilient infrastructure and foster inclusive innovation.

10

Reduced Inequalities

Reduce inequality within and among countries so opportunity is shared.

11

Sustainable Cities

Create safe, inclusive, and resilient cities and communities.

12

Responsible Consumption

Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns in daily life.

13

Climate Action

Take urgent steps to combat climate change and its impacts.

14

Life Below Water

Protect oceans, seas, and marine resources for future generations.

15

Life on Land

Safeguard forests, biodiversity, and ecosystems on land.

16

Peace, Justice & Institutions

Promote peaceful societies, provide access to justice, and build strong institutions.

17

Partnerships for the Goals

Strengthen global cooperation so every goal moves forward together.
