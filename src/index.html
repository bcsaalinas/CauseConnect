Connecting Causes with People

The 2030 Agenda is a global plan set by the United Nations with 17 goals to end poverty, fight inequality, protect the planet, and ensure prosperity for everyone. It matters because it provides a clear roadmap for creating a fairer, more sustainable world and everyone has a role to play in making it happen.

Together, we can build the future we want.
